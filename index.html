Umamusume: In Another Lifetime

Umamusume, they were born to run in a world filled with what-ifs.
But what if they weren’t running at all? What if it was all set in another universe, another timeline, another world?

Well that’s why Umamusume: In Another Lifetime is here! We create AUs of the umamusumes but in different original series/games/etc, with our current main focus being Alienstage at the moment, and you can be apart of it! Whether it be as apart of the S/VA team or part of the creative team, we need your help to bring these AUs to life.
